Our Story

2015 - Humble Beginnings

Founded by Sarah Johnson in a small rented space with just 5 weekly classes.

2017 - First Expansion

Moved to our current location and doubled our class offerings with 3 new instructors.

2019 - Community Recognition

Voted "Best Yoga Studio" in the city by local wellness magazine.

2021 - Online Platform

Launched virtual classes to serve students worldwide during the pandemic.

2023 - Wellness Center

Expanded to offer holistic wellness services including meditation and ayurvedic consultations.
